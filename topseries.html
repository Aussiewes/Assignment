Favourite Series

Top 3 K-drama

Title Plot Explaination
My Name Yoon Ji-woo seeks revenge for her father's sudden death by joining the drug crime gang Dongcheon, led by Choi Mu-jin . With Mu-jin's help, Ji-woo becomes a mole for the gang and joins the police force as an undercover agent in the drug investigation unit. Her partner in the police is Detective Jeon Pil-do .
Queen of Tears The show follows Baek Hyun-woo, a small-town lawyer, and his wife, Hong Hae-in , the CEO of a luxury department store. The couple's relationship is strained by their different lives and aspirations, but when Hae-in is diagnosed with a terminal illness, Hyun-woo reconsiders divorce and their romance rekindles
Crash Landing on You Yoon Se-ri, a successful South Korean businesswoman, crash-lands in North Korea after being blown off course by a tornado while paragliding. She meets Ri Jeong-hyeok, a North Korean army captain, who hides and protects her. Se-ri discovers the hardships of living in North Korea and falls in love with Ri Jeong-hyeok. They must find a way back to South Korea while hiding their love

Top 3 Anime

Title Plot Explaination
Attack on Titan Eren vows to destroy the Titans after they destroy his hometown and kill his mother. The series explores themes of loss, survival, and the cost of war.
Tokyo Ghoul A story about the resurrection of Kaneki and the rise of Haise Sasaki, Kaneki's new identity, in a world where ghouls and humans coexist
From Me To You Sawako Kuronuma has always been avoided by her classmates because her name, her appearance and her manner too strongly resembles that of "Sadako", the main character in the 1998 horror film Ring. They believe that when one stared into Sawako's eyes for more than three seconds, bad luck will befall the person. They also believed that she could summon ghosts. These rumors got worse because Sawako is unable to express her views straightforwardly. On the first day of school, Sawako helped Kazehaya, who was lost, to find his way to the school. Since that day, Kazehaya treats Sawako with kindness, making Sawako respect and admire him
